Quick Sort

About the Quick Sort

QuickSort is a widely used sorting algorithm that employs a divide-and-conquer strategy. It efficiently sorts elements by recursively partitioning the array and sorting its subarrays.

Approach

To solve the Quick sort problem:

  • Choose Pivot: Select a pivot element.
  • Partitioning: Rearrange elements, placing smaller ones on the left and larger ones on the right of the pivot.
  • Recursion: Apply steps 1 and 2 recursively to the left and right subarrays.
  • Base Case: Stop recursion when subarrays have 0 or 1 elements.
  • Combine: Sorted subarrays combine in place, yielding the final sorted array.
