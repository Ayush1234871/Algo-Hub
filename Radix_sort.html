Radix Sort

About Radix Sort

Radix Sort is a non-comparative sorting algorithm that works by distributing elements into buckets according to their individual digits. It can be applied to integers or strings, treating them as sequences of characters.

Approach

To implement Radix Sort:

  • Bucketize: Distribute elements into buckets based on their least significant digit.
  • Collect: Collect elements from buckets, maintaining the order.
  • Repeat: Repeat the bucketize and collect steps for each digit position, moving from the least significant to the most significant.
