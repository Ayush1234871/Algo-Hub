Division Hashing

About Division Hashing

Division Hashing is a basic hashing technique where the key is divided by the table size, and the remainder is used as the hash value.

Approach

To solve Division Hashing problem:

  1. This method involves two simple arithmetic operations: division and modulo.
  2. Given a key and a table size, divide the key by the table size.
  3. Use the remainder (result of the modulo operation) as the hash value.
