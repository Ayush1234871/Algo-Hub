Merge Sort

A classic divide-and-conquer algorithm that produces a stable, sorted output.

Best Case O(n log n)
Average Case O(n log n)
Space O(n)

🎯 About Merge Sort

Merge sort is a recursive sorting algorithm that divides an array into two halves, sorts each half independently, and then merges the sorted halves back together in ascending order.

It is one of the most respected algorithms in computer science due to its guaranteed efficiency even in the worst-case scenario and its stability (preserving the relative order of equal elements).

🚀 The Approach

1

Divide

Split the unsorted list into two approximately equal halves.

2

Conquer (Recursive)

Recursively sort both halves by continuing to divide them until they reach a size of 1.

3

Merge

Merge the two sorted halves back into a single sorted array by comparing elements one by one.

4

Combine

Repeat the merging process until you have a single, fully sorted array.
